Replacing negations with antonyms

The opposite of synonym replacement isantonym replacement. An antonym is a word that has the opposite meaning of another word. This time, instead of creating custom word mappings, we can use WordNet to replace words with unambiguous antonyms. Refer to the Looking up lemmas and synonyms in WordNet recipe in Chapter 1, Tokenizing Text and WordNet Basics, for more details on antonym lookups.
